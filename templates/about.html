About the Project

BreathCoder AI is a diagnostic tool that acts as a bridge between technology and pulmonary healthcare. Our mission is to provide early detection of lung diseases using nothing more than a standard microphone.

Why BreathCoder AI?

Traditional spirometry requires expensive equipment. BreathCoder AI uses Forced Expiratory Time (FET) and acoustic morphology to reconstruct lung flow patterns.

By identifying the specific "scooped" concave patterns in exhalation sounds, our system acts as a guide for doctors to identify early-stage obstruction.

A Clinical Tool for Doctors

"Our Explainable AI doesn't just give a score; it tells the doctor why by highlighting biological markers like FEF 25-75 and Concavity Indices."
